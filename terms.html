If you do not agree with these terms, please refrain from using our services.

1. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing or using Cleand services, including website booking, mobile applications, pickup scheduling, and store services, you agree to be legally bound by these Terms of Use.

These terms apply to all customers, visitors, and users of Cleand services.

2. Services Offered

Cleand provides professional garment care services including but not limited to:

  • Laundry services
  • Dry cleaning services
  • Shoe cleaning
  • Curtain cleaning
  • Sofa cleaning
  • Carpet and rug cleaning

Services may vary based on location and store availability.

3. Order Placement and Pickup

Customers can place orders through:

  • Website booking
  • Mobile application
  • Phone call
  • WhatsApp or store visit

The customer must provide accurate information including:

  • Name
  • Contact number
  • Pickup address
  • Garment details

Cleand reserves the right to refuse service if the provided information is incorrect or incomplete.

4. Garment Inspection Policy

All garments are inspected before cleaning to determine:

  • Fabric type
  • Stains
  • Damage
  • Care instructions

If the cleaning process cannot be carried out safely, the garment will be returned in the same condition as received.

5. Pricing and Billing

Service charges depend on:

  • Garment type
  • Fabric type
  • Cleaning method
  • Additional services requested

Final pricing may be confirmed after garment inspection.

All applicable taxes including GST will be charged as per government regulations.

6. Turnaround Time

Typical turnaround time ranges between 24 to 72 hours depending on the service type and garment category.

Express delivery services may be available at an additional cost.

Delivery timelines may vary due to:

  • Weather conditions
  • Operational constraints
  • Public holidays

7. Garment Collection Policy

Customers must collect their garments within a reasonable period after notification.

Garments must be collected within 60 days from the date of deposit.

Cleand will attempt to notify customers through SMS or digital communication when garments are ready for collection.

However, Cleand cannot be held responsible for garments that remain uncollected beyond the defined notification period.

8. Stain Removal Disclaimer

While Cleand uses professional techniques for stain removal:

Some stains may be permanent depending on:

  • Fabric type
  • Stain age
  • Chemical exposure
  • Heat damage

Cleand does not guarantee removal of all stains.

9. Delicate and High-Value Garments

Garments such as:

  • Silk
  • Designer wear
  • Wedding attire
  • Leather garments
  • Garments with heavy embellishments
  • require specialized treatment.

Customers are advised to inform Cleand about any special care instructions before cleaning.

10. Liability and Compensation

Cleand takes utmost care during the cleaning process.

However, Cleand’s liability in case of proven damage caused during cleaning will be limited to compensation as per company policy.

Compensation will be determined based on:

  • Garment condition
  • Age of garment
  • Market value
  • Cleaning risks associated with the fabric

Cleand is not responsible for damage resulting from:

  • Manufacturing defects
  • Fabric weakness
  • Color bleeding
  • Improper prior washing

11. Garment Tagging and Identification

Every garment is tagged during processing.

Customers are advised to verify garment count at the time of pickup and delivery.

The number and type of garments listed on the receipt will be considered final for verification purposes.

12. Prohibited Items

Customers must not submit the following items for cleaning:

  • Items contaminated with hazardous chemicals
  • Items infested with insects
  • Items containing sharp objects
  • Items with biological contamination

Cleand reserves the right to reject such items.

13. Payment Terms

Payment may be made through:

  • UPI
  • Credit card
  • Debit card
  • Net banking
  • Cash
  • Wallet balance

Payment terms may vary depending on store policies.

14. Membership and Promotional Offers

Cleand may offer membership programs, wallet credits, or promotional discounts.

Such offers:

  • May be time-limited
  • May apply only to selected services
  • Cannot be combined with other promotions unless specified.

15. Customer Responsibilities

Customers must:

  • Check pockets before handing garments
  • Declare delicate fabrics
  • Provide correct contact details
  • Collect garments within the specified period

Cleand will not be responsible for items left in pockets.

Users agree not to misuse the Cleand website or mobile application.

Prohibited activities include:
