Clothing is personal

Garment Care, Reimagined

It carries the moments of our lives. The meetings that matter, the celebrations we remember, the everyday confidence we carry with us.

Yet for decades, garment care has remained one of the most fragmented and inconsistent services in urban life.

Cleand was built to change that.

Cleand The Laundry Spa is redefining garment care by bringing together technology, operational precision, and professional service standards. Our goal is simple but ambitious: to make garment care effortless, reliable, and consistently exceptional.

We believe that a service people use every week deserves the same innovation, design, and reliability that modern consumers expect from the world’s best brands.

Cleand exists to deliver that experience.

A Message from the Founder & CEO

Bringing Professionalism to Garment Care

When I started Cleand, the goal was never just to open a laundry store. The vision was to bring professionalism to an industry that had remained largely unorganized for decades.

During my corporate career, I had the opportunity to work with some of India’s most respected companies and leaders. I learned how systems, technology, and customer obsession can transform industries. I believed the same transformation could happen in garment care.

Leaving a stable corporate career to start Cleand was not the easy path. The early days required persistence, learning, and constant improvement. But every satisfied customer reinforced the belief that people were ready for a better service experience.

Today, Cleand represents a commitment to quality, reliability, and convenience for customers who trust us with something as personal as their clothing.

Our journey is still in its early chapters. The opportunity to build a national brand in garment care is immense, and we are committed to building Cleand into one of India’s most trusted service companies.

Amit S

Founder and CEO
Cleand The Laundry Spa

Our Story

Cleand began with a simple observation

Laundry services were everywhere, yet consistent quality and professional service were rare. The industry lacked structure, technology, and brand trust.

Cleand was created to bring these elements together

By combining modern operational systems, trained professionals, and digital convenience, Cleand introduced a new category of service called the Laundry Spa. A place where garments receive professional care instead of routine washing.

This idea has gradually evolved into a growing network of services designed to simplify garment care for modern households.

The Cleand Journey

2017

The Vision

The idea for Cleand was conceived with the vision of professionalizing garment care services.

2018

The First Spa

The first Cleand store was launched, introducing the concept of a Laundry Spa.

2019

Expanding Horizons

Service offerings expanded to include dry cleaning, curtain, sofa, shoe, and carpet cleaning.

2020

Digital Transformation

Technology integration enabled pickup/delivery services and digital order management.

2022

Company Owned Growth

Cleand began expanding through its Company-owned Stores.

2024

Franchise Era

Cleand began expanding through franchise partnerships across cities.

Today

Looking Ahead

Cleand continues to grow while strengthening its technology and operational systems.

Why Cleand is Different?

Scalable Business Model

Our Roadmap

Cleand Vision 2030

The garment care industry in India is evolving rapidly as urban lifestyles demand convenience and hygiene. Cleand’s long term vision is to build a nationwide network of professional garment care centers supported by technology, logistics, and standardized processes.

By 2030, Cleand aims to:

  • Expand across major Indian cities
  • Build one of the largest professional laundry networks in the country
  • Create opportunities for entrepreneurs through franchise partnerships
  • Serve millions of garments every month through reliable and consistent service

The ambition is not simply growth. The ambition is to build a brand that customers trust for decades.

Our Core Values

Building Trust Through Service

Cleand has steadily earned the confidence of customers who rely on consistent quality and dependable garment care. Our growing community of customers and franchise partners reflects the trust placed in the Cleand brand.

Every garment cleaned and every order delivered strengthens that relationship.

Trust is not built through promises.

It is Built Through Services
